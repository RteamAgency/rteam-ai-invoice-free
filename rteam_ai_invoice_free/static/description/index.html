Rteam AI Invoice Free

AI-powered vendor invoice extraction from PDF and JPG files, right inside Odoo.

Version 18.0.1.0.0  |  License: LGPL-3  |  Free tier: 5 extractions/month

What it does

  • Upload a PDF or JPG vendor invoice directly from a draft vendor bill.
  • AI extracts vendor name, VAT, invoice number, dates, currency, and line items.
  • Each extracted field shows a confidence indicator (High / Medium / Low).
  • Smart matching: finds existing vendor by VAT or name; matches purchase taxes by rate.
  • Review the preview and click Confirm to pre-fill the draft vendor bill.
  • Free tier: 5 extractions per company per month. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited use.

What it does NOT do

  • No approval workflow or 3-way PO match (Pro tier).
  • No email-in or batch 10+ files (Pro tier).
  • No auto-post of invoices.

Configuration

Go to Accounting > Configuration > Settings to see monthly quota usage and configure the gateway URL.
